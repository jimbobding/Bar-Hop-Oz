Bar Hop UK

About

Bar Hop UK is your friend in the quest to find good beer in the UK. we believe in good beer and we believe in supporting local business. We give you the tools to find local breweries in your area or in other areas you may want to visit. We also now that drinking alone is not always what it is cracked to up to be and are offering drinking tours around selected breweries. Allowing you to experience local craft breweries and meet new people.
